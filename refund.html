1. Order Cancellation

You may cancel your order before it has been shipped. To request cancellation, email us at masalamehta@gmail.com.

2. No Cancellations After Dispatch

Once the order has been dispatched, cancellation is not allowed as we deal with consumable food products.

3. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are granted only if:

  • Wrong product received
  • Damaged product upon delivery
  • Missing items
  • Package lost in transit

Customers must contact us within 48 hours of delivery and provide clear photos of the product and packaging.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Opened or used products
  • Taste preferences
  • Incorrect address provided by customer
  • Courier delays

5. Refund Timeline

Approved refunds will be processed via Razorpay to the original payment method within 5–7 working days.
